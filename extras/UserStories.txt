The different types of users for the app are readers and writers of detective stories.


As a reader of a detective story, I would like to…
- get clues about the details of the murder,
- get clues about the identity of the murderer,
- guess the identity of the murderer, and see the probability that my guess is correct.


As a writer of detective stories, I would like to…
- get an idea for a story plot,
- given elements of a story, see attributes of the predicted murderer for that story.
